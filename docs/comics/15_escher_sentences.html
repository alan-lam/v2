Escher Sentences

More people have been to Russia than I have. Source: Reddit

If you're a prepositionist, you probably meant to say: More people than me have been to Russia. Well, Captain Obvious, you said "I", not "me". So you're wrong!

If you're a conjunctionist, you probably meant to say: Other people have been to Russia more times than I have. Well, you said "more people", not "more times". So you're wrong!
